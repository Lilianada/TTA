Who's Behind this Website

My name is Lilian Ada, I am a Twenty-One year old girl. I help under 30's understand the issues that comes with adulting while also providing Entry-level Frontend Developers with resources and tools for learning HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
I created this website as a tool to reach the outside world, connect with people just like me and add a bit of value, here and there.
I like to call myself The Confused Creative because I know I can create but I don't know what to focus on. You can check my Portfolio for what I think I know how to do best for the time being.
