Who's Behind this Website

My name is Lilian Ada, I am a Twenty-One year old African girl I barely just got out of the university still trying to find a little balance. I created this website as my tool to reach the outside world and connect with people just like me.

What we're About

TalktoAda is a playful website with an original voice. As a business, TalktoAda is cheerful, lighthearted and accessible. People perceive her as unique and trendy. This website was created with the intent of sharing real life experiences of a teenage black girl with all the ups and downs of navigating adulthood. It goes further to provide you with helpful resources for Beginners in Frontend Development, also providing fun and cool materials for purchase.
